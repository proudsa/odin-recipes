Chocolate Fudge - The Non-dairy way

Peri Peri sauce is just not "the everyday" condiment, use it as a marinade, a dressing, a dipping sauce or a basting sauce. Best enjoyed with friends.

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup cacao power
  • 1 cup dates - pitted
  • 1 cup coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla - powder
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Cover the dates in water and allow to soften (use warm water to speed up this process)
  2. Add everything together in a food processor and process until completely smooth and mixed. This takes up to 20 minutes, and is well worth spending the time on this part.
  3. Pour into a shallow dish and allow it to set in the fridge. After about 3-4 hours, cut into squares.

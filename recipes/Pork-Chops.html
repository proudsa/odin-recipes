Pork Chops the Apex way

This recipe is taken from the "computer game" APEX Legends. It is his family recipes. This site shows 3 different version for this recipe, made by Binging With Babish. The recipe below is the "in game" version. Click this Reddit site for discussions on the recipe from feloow gamers.

Ingredients

  • 80ml Djion mustard
  • 100ml Honey
  • 30ml Maple syrup
  • 15g Cinnamon
  • Light cayenne
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Seat in hot oil
  2. Smother in glaze
  3. Pop in oven at 190C for 10-12min

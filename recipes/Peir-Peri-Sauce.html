Peri Peri Sauce the quick way

Peri Peri sauce is just not "the everyday" condiment, use it as a marinade, a dressing, a dipping sauce or a basting sauce. Best enjoyed with friends.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • ½ cup freshly squeezed lemon or lime
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 1 tot chilli powder (25 ml)
  • 1 tot paprika (25 ml)
  • 1 tot salt (25 ml)
  • 1 tot chopped garlic (25 ml)
  • 10 African bird’s eye chillies

Steps

You can chop the carlic and chillies coarse or as fine as you like. For a real smooth texture put all ingerdients into a blender. It all depends on the consistency of the sauce you want.

  1. Chop or blend ingredients
  2. Put into a jar
  3. Put the lid on the jar, shake well and your done

The sauce improves with time and will be even better and more fiery after standing for an hour or a day. If you want the sauce hotter, add more chillies and chilli powder.
